##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining robust API integrations with various financial institutions, payment processors, and third-party services. You'll work closely with our engineering team to ensure scalable, secure, and reliable API infrastructure that supports our accounting automation features.

This role involves deep technical work with financial APIs, security considerations, and ensuring data integrity across multiple integrations.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong experience with API design and development
- Proficiency in modern programming languages (Python, Node.js, or similar)
- Experience with RESTful APIs and GraphQL
- Understanding of authentication and security best practices
- Experience with database design and optimization

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with financial services APIs
- Knowledge of accounting and financial data standards
- Experience with rate limiting and error handling
- Background in fintech or financial technology
- Understanding of OAuth, JWT, and other security protocols

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent attention to detail
- Customer-oriented approach
- Strong communication skills

---

##### What we offer:

- A full-time position with competitive compensation
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Training in accounting and financial technology
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
