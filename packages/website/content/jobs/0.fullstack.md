##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be developing new features across our full stack, integrating with various financial APIs, building robust backend systems, and creating intuitive frontend interfaces. There's also always a lot of troubleshooting user reported issues, bugs, and feature requests. You can improve our current implementation and help us get Otutu to the next level!

As a growing platform, we work collaboratively on our codebase and take pride in building professional, scalable solutions. This is an exciting opportunity to shape the future of financial automation.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong experience with modern web technologies (Node.js, TypeScript, Python, or similar)
- Fullstack development experience with both frontend and backend expertise
- Experience with database design and API integration
- Understanding of accounting principles and financial systems (preferred but not required)

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with financial software or fintech platforms
- Understanding of double-entry bookkeeping principles
- Experience with testing and quality assurance
- Knowledge of security best practices for financial applications
- Background in accounting or finance

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Can work independently but cooperates well with other team members
- Proactive and customer-oriented
- Strong communication skills in English

---

##### What we offer:

- A full-time position with competitive compensation
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Training in accounting and financial technology
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
