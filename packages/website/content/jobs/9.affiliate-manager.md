##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for building and managing our affiliate and referral program to drive organic growth. You'll recruit, onboard, and manage partners who can refer potential customers to Otutu, including accounting professionals, financial advisors, business consultants, and influencers in the accounting space.

This role involves developing partnership strategies, managing relationships with affiliates, tracking performance metrics, and optimizing conversion rates.
