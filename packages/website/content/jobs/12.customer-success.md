##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for ensuring our customers achieve their goals with Otutu's accounting platform. You'll work proactively with users to understand their needs, help them maximize value from our platform, and drive customer retention and expansion. This role involves building strong relationships, providing strategic guidance, and acting as the voice of the customer within our organization.
