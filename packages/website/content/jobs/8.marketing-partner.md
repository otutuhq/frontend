##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for developing and executing comprehensive marketing strategies to increase brand awareness and drive user acquisition. You'll work closely with our product and sales teams to create compelling content, campaigns, and partnerships that resonate with accounting professionals and business owners.

This role involves digital marketing, content creation, social media strategy, and building relationships with industry influencers.
