##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for designing exceptional user experiences for our accounting platform, working closely with our product and engineering teams. You'll focus on creating intuitive interfaces, optimizing user flows, and ensuring our platform is both powerful and accessible to accounting professionals and business owners.

This contract role involves designing new features, improving existing workflows, and collaborating on product strategy to create the best possible user experience.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong portfolio demonstrating product design expertise
- Experience designing B2B or financial software interfaces
- Proficiency with design tools (Figma, Sketch, or similar)
- Understanding of user-centered design principles
- Ability to create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with accounting software or fintech design
- Knowledge of accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Understanding of design systems and component libraries
- Experience with user research and usability testing
- Background in professional services or financial workflows

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- Strong collaboration and communication skills
- Ability to work independently on contract basis
- Understanding of business needs and technical constraints
- Proactive approach to problem-solving
- Ability to present design rationale and decisions

---

##### What we offer:

- Competitive contract rates
- Flexible contract arrangement
- Opportunity to work on innovative financial technology
- Collaborative design process
- Credit for design work in portfolio
