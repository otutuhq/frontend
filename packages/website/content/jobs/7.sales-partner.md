##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for identifying, developing, and closing strategic partnerships with accounting firms, financial advisors, and business consultants. You'll work closely with our business development team to build relationships that drive mutual value and help scale our platform.

This role involves understanding the needs of accounting professionals, presenting Otutu's value proposition, and negotiating partnership agreements that benefit both parties.

---

##### Requirements:

- Proven track record in B2B sales and partnership development
- Experience in financial services or fintech sales
- Strong relationship building and negotiation skills
- Understanding of accounting industry and professional services
- Excellent communication and presentation skills

---

##### Bonus points:

- Network of accounting professionals or financial advisors
