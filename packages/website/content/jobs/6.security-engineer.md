##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for ensuring the security and integrity of our accounting platform. You'll work on implementing security best practices, conducting security audits, managing vulnerabilities, and ensuring compliance with financial security standards. This role is critical in maintaining trust with our customers who entrust us with their financial data.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong experience with application security and security engineering
- Knowledge of security testing methodologies (SAST, DAST, penetration testing)
- Understanding of authentication, authorization, and security protocols
- Experience with secure coding practices and security frameworks
- Knowledge of financial security standards and compliance

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with fintech or financial services security
- Knowledge of regulatory requirements (PCI DSS, SOX, etc.)
- Experience with security monitoring and incident response
- Background in cybersecurity or information security
- Understanding of accounting data protection requirements

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent attention to detail
- Strong communication skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

---

##### What we offer:

- A full-time position with competitive compensation
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Training in financial security and compliance
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
