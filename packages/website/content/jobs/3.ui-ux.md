##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### What are we looking for

We are looking for a Senior UI/UX Designer who wants to be part of our team and aligns with our mission to build exceptional financial technology solutions. You will help us improve our application's user experience and design for accounting professionals and business users who need powerful yet intuitive financial management tools.

The position is fully remote and focuses on creating professional, trustworthy interfaces for critical financial workflows.

---

##### What will working with us look like?

Your primary focus will be working with the team to improve the overall application user experience for accounting workflows. You will brainstorm, collect and evaluate user feedback from accountants and financial professionals, iterate and translate it to user flows, wireframes, prototypes and mockups that the Laravel/Filament team can implement.

Your secondary focus will be defining and building a brand identity around our Otutu brand, website UX improvement and the creation of promotional material, such as professional graphics and other marketing materials.

---

##### Responsibilities

- Create and define user centric design SOPs for financial software
- Define and create current and new personas for accounting professionals and business users
- Study and define current user flows for complex financial workflows and create improved ones
- Collect, understand and prioritize feedback from accountants, bookkeepers, and financial stakeholders
- Help the team brainstorm new features and improve existing ones in a user centric way
- Create user flows, wireframes, prototypes and mockups for accounting interfaces
- Design financial dashboards, reporting interfaces, and professional accounting tools
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards for financial software
- Collaborate with Laravel/Filament developers to ensure design implementation quality

---

##### Requirements

- **Design Expertise:**
  - 4+ years of UI/UX design experience, preferably with B2B or financial software
  - Proficiency in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite (XD, Illustrator, Photoshop)
  - Strong portfolio demonstrating complex interface design and user flow creation
  - Experience with design systems and component libraries

- **Financial Software Experience:**
  - Understanding of accounting workflows, financial data presentation, and professional software interfaces
  - Experience designing for accountants, bookkeepers, or financial professionals
  - Knowledge of double-entry bookkeeping concepts and financial terminology
  - Familiarity with ERP, accounting software, or financial dashboards

- **Technical Skills:**
  - Experience designing for web applications using modern frameworks
  - Understanding of responsive design and mobile-first approaches
  - Knowledge of accessibility standards (WCAG) and inclusive design
  - Experience with user research, usability testing, and A/B testing
  - Familiarity with agile development processes and design handoff

- **Professional Skills:**
  - Excellent communication skills for collaborating with technical teams
  - Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities
  - Customer-focused mindset with attention to user needs
  - Ability to work independently and as part of a distributed team
  - Experience presenting design concepts to stakeholders
