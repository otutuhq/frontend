##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### What are we looking for

We are looking for a UI/UX designer who wants to be part of our team and aligns with our mission to build exceptional financial technology solutions. You will help us improve our application's user experience and design for accounting professionals and business users who need powerful yet intuitive financial management tools.

The position is fully remote and it can be either part-time or full-time.

---

##### What will working with us look like?

Your primary focus will be working with the team to improve the overall application user experience for accounting workflows. You will brainstorm, collect and evaluate user feedback, iterate and translate it to user flows, wireframes, prototypes and mockups that the frontend team can use for implementation.

Your secondary focus will be defining and building a brand identity around our Otutu brand, website UX improvement and the creation of promotional material, such as professional graphics and other marketing materials.

---

##### Responsibilities

- Create and define user centric design SOPs
- Define and create current and new personas for accounting professionals
- Study and define current user flows for financial workflows and create new ones
- Collect, understand and prioritize feedback from users and stakeholders
- Help the team brainstorm new ideas and improve existing ones in a user centric way
- Create user flows, wireframes, prototypes and mockups for accounting interfaces
- Creating original designs for financial dashboards and professional interfaces

---

##### Requirements

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Understanding of financial applications and professional software interfaces
- Experience with design systems and component libraries
