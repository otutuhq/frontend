##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for managing Otutu's accounting operations, supporting our revenue-share partnerships with accounting professionals, and ensuring accurate financial reporting as we scale. You'll work closely with our finance team, partnership managers, and accounting professionals to maintain compliance and optimize our financial operations.

This role involves managing complex revenue-share calculations, supporting partnership accounting, maintaining accurate financial records, and ensuring our platform's financial operations align with accounting best practices.

---

##### Technical Requirements:

- **Accounting Expertise:**
  - Professional accounting certification (CPA, ACCA, or equivalent)
  - 5+ years of accounting operations experience
  - Advanced double-entry bookkeeping knowledge
  - Accrual accounting and financial reporting expertise
  - GAAP/IFRS compliance understanding

- **Revenue Operations:**
  - Complex revenue-share and partnership accounting
  - Multi-entity accounting and consolidation
  - Subscription revenue recognition and management
  - Financial modeling and forecasting
  - Partnership agreement financial management

- **Financial Systems:**
  - Accounting software expertise (QuickBooks, Xero, or similar)
  - ERP system experience and integration
  - Financial data analysis and reporting tools
  - Budget planning and variance analysis
  - Audit preparation and compliance support

- **Financial Operations:**
  - Month-end and year-end close processes
  - Financial statement preparation and analysis
  - Tax compliance and regulatory reporting
  - Internal controls and process documentation
  - Risk assessment and financial controls
