##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for supporting our accounting operations and ensuring smooth revenue-share scaling. You'll work closely with our finance team to manage accounting processes, support partnership revenue-share models, and help maintain accurate financial records as we scale our platform.

This role involves managing financial operations, supporting our revenue-share partnerships with accounting professionals, and ensuring our platform maintains the highest accounting standards.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong accounting background with professional certification (CPA, ACCA, or equivalent)
- Experience with double-entry bookkeeping and accrual accounting
- Understanding of revenue-share and partnership accounting models
- Experience with financial reporting and compliance requirements
- Strong analytical and attention-to-detail skills

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with fintech or SaaS accounting operations
- Knowledge of automated accounting systems and software
- Background in partnership accounting and revenue recognition
- Experience with cross-border financial operations
- Understanding of financial technology regulations

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Proactive approach to identifying accounting improvements
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

---

##### What we offer:

- Competitive salary with performance-based bonuses
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Professional development and accounting training opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
- Opportunity to shape accounting practices for cutting-edge financial technology
