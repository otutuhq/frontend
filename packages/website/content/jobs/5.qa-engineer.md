##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of our accounting platform through comprehensive testing strategies. You'll work closely with our engineering and product teams to develop test plans, execute test cases, and identify potential issues before they reach our customers.

This role involves testing financial calculations, API integrations, user workflows, and ensuring compliance with accounting standards.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong experience with software testing methodologies
- Experience with automated testing tools and frameworks
- Understanding of API testing and validation
- Attention to detail and analytical thinking
- Experience with bug tracking and test documentation

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with financial software testing
- Knowledge of accounting principles and financial data
- Experience with performance testing and security testing
- Background in fintech or financial technology
- Understanding of regulatory compliance requirements

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Strong communication skills
- Proactive problem-solving approach
- Customer-focused mindset
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

---

##### What we offer:

- A full-time position with competitive compensation
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Training in accounting and financial technology
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
