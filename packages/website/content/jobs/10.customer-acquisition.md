##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for developing and executing customer acquisition strategies to drive user growth and conversions. You'll work closely with our marketing and product teams to optimize conversion funnels, improve onboarding experiences, and scale our user base efficiently.

This role involves analyzing user behavior, A/B testing, optimizing marketing channels, and working with data to identify growth opportunities.
