##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

We are looking for a passionate Vue.js developer with excellent knowledge of TypeScript. Your primary focus will be to assist in implementing new features, improving the frontend quality and performance. You will also assist in modularizing and improving our Vue.js application to make it scalable and maintainable. The position requires an understanding of financial workflows and data to implement features that will provide the best experience to our accounting professional users.

---

##### Technical requirements:

- Excellent knowledge of TypeScript
- Excellent knowledge of Vue.js & Vuex (or pinia)
- Experience with automated JavaScript testing (Jest, vitest, Cypress)
- Experience with consuming RESTful APIs
- Any experience building modular Vue.js applications with reusable components

---

##### Bonus points:

- DevOps experience
- Experience with financial software or accounting platforms
- Understanding of double-entry bookkeeping principles
