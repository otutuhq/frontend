##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for providing technical support to our customers, helping them troubleshoot issues, and ensuring they get the most value from Otutu's accounting platform. You'll work closely with our engineering team to resolve technical issues and communicate effectively with users who may not have deep technical backgrounds.

This role involves investigating complex technical issues, understanding customer workflows, and providing clear solutions while maintaining high customer satisfaction.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong technical background with ability to understand complex systems
- Experience with customer support in a technical role
- Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal)
- Understanding of accounting and financial data
- Ability to troubleshoot and problem-solve effectively

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with accounting software support
- Background in fintech or financial technology
- Knowledge of API troubleshooting and integration issues
- Experience with database queries and data analysis
- Understanding of financial compliance and regulations

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Patient and empathetic approach to customer issues
- Strong organizational skills
- Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical users
- Proactive approach to identifying and preventing issues

---

##### What we offer:

- Base salary with performance-based bonuses
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and supportive team environment
- Competitive benefits package
