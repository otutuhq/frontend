##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for helping scale our operations, optimize business processes, and ensure smooth revenue-share scaling. You'll work across multiple departments to improve efficiency, implement systems, and support our growth trajectory. This role involves analyzing operational metrics, identifying improvement opportunities, and implementing solutions that enable sustainable scaling.

This position is critical for maintaining operational excellence as we grow our customer base and expand our services.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Experience with operational excellence and process improvement
- Understanding of business metrics and performance indicators
- Ability to work with cross-functional teams
- Strong organizational and project management skills

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with fintech or SaaS operations
- Knowledge of revenue-share business models
- Background in financial services operations
- Experience with automation and workflow optimization

- Proactive approach to identifying operational improvements
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Attention to detail and quality focus
