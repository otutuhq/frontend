##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for ensuring Otutu's operations comply with all applicable laws and regulations, particularly in the financial services and accounting sectors. You'll work closely with our leadership team to navigate legal requirements, draft agreements, and ensure our platform and business practices meet regulatory standards.

This role involves staying current with financial regulations, supporting revenue-share partnerships, and ensuring smooth legal operations as we scale our services.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong legal background with experience in financial services law
- Understanding of regulatory compliance requirements
- Experience with technology and SaaS legal frameworks
- Knowledge of data protection and privacy laws
- Ability to draft and review legal documents and agreements

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with fintech or accounting software legal issues
- Knowledge of revenue-share and partnership legal structures
- Background in international financial regulations
- Experience with financial services licensing requirements
- Understanding of cross-border compliance issues

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Strong analytical and research skills
- Excellent attention to detail
- Strong communication and negotiation skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

---

##### What we offer:

- Competitive salary with performance-based bonuses
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Professional development and legal training opportunities
- Collaborative and supportive work environment
- Opportunity to shape legal framework for innovative financial technology
