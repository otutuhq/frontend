##### What is Otutu?

Otutu is an accounting platform featuring full double-entry accrual accounting, delivering modern automation for professional financial management. We're building the next generation of financial technology solutions that help businesses and individuals manage their finances with precision and automation.

---

##### How your work with us will look like?

You will be responsible for providing first-line technical support to our customers, helping them resolve issues, answer questions, and get the most out of Otutu's accounting platform. You'll work closely with our engineering and customer success teams to escalate complex issues and ensure smooth customer experiences.

This role involves troubleshooting technical issues, guiding users through platform features, and maintaining high customer satisfaction through excellent support.

---

##### Requirements:

- Strong problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
- Experience with customer support and technical assistance
- Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal)
- Understanding of accounting and financial workflows
- Ability to work efficiently with support tools and systems

---

##### Bonus points:

- Experience with accounting software support
- Background in fintech or financial technology
- Knowledge of API integrations and third-party connections
- Experience with database queries and data management
- Understanding of financial compliance and reporting

---

##### Non-technical bonus points:

- 4 hours daily overlap with European working hours for smoother teamwork
- Patient and helpful approach to customer inquiries
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Ability to explain complex concepts in simple terms
- Proactive approach to identifying common issues and solutions

---

##### What we offer:

- Base salary with performance-based bonuses
- Flexible working hours and remote work
- Professional development opportunities
- Supportive and collaborative team environment
- Competitive benefits package
